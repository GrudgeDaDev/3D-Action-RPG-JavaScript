## üìä Progression Overview

### Experience Formula
```
XP Required = 100 √ó (Level ^ 1.5)
```

### Level Milestones
- **Level 1-10:** Basic recipes, learning the profession
- **Level 10-25:** Improved efficiency, better materials
- **Level 25-50:** Advanced recipes, combat stat bonuses
- **Level 50-75:** Legendary recipes, special abilities
- **Level 75-100:** Mythic crafting, ultimate powers

### Rarity Tiers
- **Common (Gray):** Level 1-10 recipes
- **Uncommon (Green):** Level 10-25 recipes
- **Rare (Blue):** Level 25-50 recipes
- **Epic (Purple):** Level 50-75 recipes
- **Legendary (Orange):** Level 75-90 recipes
- **Mythic (Gold):** Level 90-100 recipes

## üéÆ Quick Tips

1. **Start with gathering professions** (Mining, Fishing) to collect materials
2. **Level multiple professions** for synergy (Mining + Blacksmithing)
3. **Focus on one profession** to reach level 100 faster
4. **Craft items you need** rather than just for XP
5. **Ultimate abilities** are game-changers at level 100
6. **Combat stat bonuses** from professions stack with equipment
